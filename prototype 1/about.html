Our Heritage

The Vohda Story

Founded on the principle of discreet excellence, Vohda Hotels & Resorts began as a private family estate in 1924. Today, we continue that legacy of intimate luxury across the globe.

The Vohda Philosophy

We believe that luxury is not loud. It is the silent anticipation of your needs, the tactile quality of Belgian linens, and the profound stillness of a curated environment.

Our approach, termed "The Art of Absence," focuses on removing the friction of modern life. By creating spaces that breathe, we allow our guests to rediscover their own rhythm. Every Vohda property is designed to be a sanctuary of poise and unhurried grace.
