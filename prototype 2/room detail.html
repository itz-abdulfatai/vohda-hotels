The Penthouse Suite

square_foot 1,200 sq ft group Up to 3 Guests bed 1 King Bed panorama City View

Perched atop the Vohda, The Penthouse Suite offers an unparalleled sanctuary of quiet luxury. Designed for the discerning traveler, this expansive space blends bespoke furnishings with panoramic city vistas. Every detail, from the hand-loomed rugs to the curated artwork, has been selected to create an environment of profound serenity and comfort.

The suite features a spacious separate living area ideal for intimate gatherings or focused work, a spa-inspired marble bathroom complete with a deep soaking tub, and a private terrace that provides a secluded vantage point over the skyline. Experience a level of service and design that redefines modern hospitality.
